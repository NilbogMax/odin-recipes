Firecracker Chicken

Description

Firecracker chicken is very similar to sweet and sour chicken, only much spicier (hence the name). Browning the chicken keeps it moist and tender and if you serve it over rice, the heat is mellowed out.

Ingredients:
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ginger
  • Hot Sauce
  • Rice
  • Chicken Breasts
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Combine cornstarch, ginger, pepper, and 1 teaspoon salt in a large resealable bag or large bowl. Place beaten eggs in a large shallow bowl or dish. Add chicken to bag and shake to coat.
  3. Using a slotted spoon, scoop out chicken, allowing excess egg to drip back into the bowl. Place in the hot skillet in an even layer. Cook, turning once, until chicken is golden on both sides, about 2 minutes per side (chicken will not be cooked through at this point)
  4. Serve over rice and sprinkle with scallions and sesame seeds.
